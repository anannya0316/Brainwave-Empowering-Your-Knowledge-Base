💸Rising indebtedness among cultivators reflects complex social changes and disintegration of local communities. 
⚖️Caste, land, and economic disparities persist but are not solely defined by traditional jajmani relations. 
🌍Aspirations to move beyond agriculture are increasing as villages integrate into regional, national, and global economies. 
📝Social science perspectives and policy preoccupations have evolved over time, necessitating revisits to the study of Indian villages. 
📚This book presents 14 essays based on empirical research conducted around or after the turn of the century.
